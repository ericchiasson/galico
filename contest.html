Hot Water Tank Giveaway

Galico Home Comfort Inc. – Hot Water Tank Giveaway
Official Contest Rules

No Purchase Necessary. Ontario Only.

1. Sponsor

The contest is sponsored by Galico Home Comfort Inc. (“Galico”), located in Almonte, Ontario, Canada.

2. Contest Period

The contest begins on November 15, 2025 and ends on December 31, 2025 (the “Contest Period”).
Weekly qualifying rounds will occur every Saturday during the Contest Period.
A final winner will be selected at the end of the Contest Period.

3. Eligibility

The contest is open to legal residents of Ontario who:

  • Are 18 years of age or older
  • Are homeowners
  • Live within Arnprior, Beckwith, Carleton Place, Mississippi Mills, Ottawa, Perth, or Smiths Falls
  • Submit all required entry information accurately

Employees of Galico, contractors, agents, and immediate family members are not eligible.

4. How to Enter

During the Contest Period:

  • Complete the official entry form (online via website or Facebook Lead Form).
  • Provide your:
    • Name
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Address or postal code
  • Answer the weekly qualifier mathematical question correctly.

Each person may enter once per contest. Duplicate or fraudulent entries are void.

5. Contest Structure

Weekly Qualifier Selection (“Qualifier Round”)

Each week, all valid entries received during that week will be reviewed.

  • Participants must answer a mathematical skill-based question correctly on the entry form.
  • All participants who answer correctly are placed into a weekly pool.
  • Galico will randomly select one weekly qualifier (“Weekly Qualifier”) from that week’s pool.

No prize is awarded to Weekly Qualifiers. Weekly Qualifiers simply advance to the Final Winner Selection.

Final Winner Selection

From all Weekly Qualifiers who correctly answer the mathematical skill-based quiz question:

  • Galico will conduct a random draw to choose one potential winner.
  • Before being confirmed as the winner, the selected entrant must:
    • Correctly answer a second mathematical skill-testing question, administered by phone or email.
    • Respond to the notification email within 48 hours.

If the selected entrant fails the skill-testing question, misses the deadline, or refuses the publicity requirements, they will be disqualified and another eligible Weekly Qualifier will be drawn.

6. Prize

There is one (1) Grand Prize consisting of:

  • One (1) new hot water tank, brand and model selected by Galico
  • Professional installation by a licensed Galico technician

Total approximate value: $2,500
Prize has no cash value, is non-transferable, and cannot be substituted.

7. Publicity & Winner Requirements

As a condition of winning, the final confirmed winner MUST agree to:

  • Have their full name publicly announced on Galico’s website and social media accounts
  • Have a photo taken with a Galico technician and the installed water heater
  • Grant Galico the right to use the winner’s name, image, and photo for:
    • Advertising
    • Social media posts
    • Website usage
    • Marketing materials

This publicity release is mandatory. Refusal to participate results in disqualification, and another winner will be selected.

8. Disqualification

Galico reserves the right to disqualify any entrant who:

  • Provides false or incomplete information
  • Fails to answer the weekly qualifier skill-testing question correctly
  • Fails to answer the final skill-testing question correctly
  • Refuses to comply with the publicity requirement
  • Attempts to manipulate or tamper with the contest process

9. Minimum Entry Requirement & Right to Extend Contest Period

A minimum of 200 eligible entries must be received during the Contest Period for the Grand Prize draw to take place. If this minimum entry threshold is not reached, the Sponsor reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to extend the Contest Period for a reasonable amount of time to allow additional eligible entries to be received. Any such extension will be announced on the Sponsor’s website and/or social media channels. The Grand Prize will only be awarded once at least 200 eligible entries have been received.

10. Privacy

By entering, participants consent to Galico’s collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for contest administration, in accordance with Galico’s Privacy Policy.

11. Limitation of Liability

Galico is not responsible for:

  • Technical errors
  • Lost or misdirected entries
  • Internet outages
  • System failures
  • Email delivery issues

By entering, participants release Galico from all liability related to the contest and prize.

12. Governing Law

The contest is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario.

13. Acceptance of Rules

Entering the contest constitutes full acceptance of these Official Rules.
