Gas Quick Connector

Enjoy the convenience and safety of a permanent, code-compliant gas connection for your outdoor BBQ and portable generator with professional gas quick connector installation from Galico, proudly serving Ottawa, Carleton Place, Arnprior, Perth, and Smiths Falls. Say goodbye to propane tank refills and messy hoses—our certified technicians install 3/8" or 1/2" gas quick-disconnect fittings directly to your natural gas or propane line, giving you a clean, reliable, and instantly accessible fuel source for your grill or backup generator. Whether you’re building a new outdoor kitchen, upgrading your patio setup, or preparing for emergency power outages, we ensure your quick-connect system is properly sized, leak-tested, and compliant with Ontario gas codes. We use CSA-approved brass fittings, install shut-off valves for safety, and position connectors for easy access and weather protection. As TSSA licensed gas technicians, we understand local regulations and climate considerations—so your outdoor gas connections remain safe and functional year-round. Trust Galico for fast, clean, and certified BBQ and generator gas quick connector installations that enhance your home’s functionality and safety. Contact us today to schedule your service!
