Smart Thermostat Installation

Upgrade your home’s comfort and efficiency with professional smart thermostat installation from Galico, proudly serving Ottawa, Carleton Place, Arnprior, Perth, and Smiths Falls. Whether you’re looking to lower your energy bills, gain remote control of your heating and cooling, or integrate your HVAC system with smart home platforms like Google Home, Amazon Alexa, or Apple HomeKit, our certified technicians ensure seamless, code-compliant installation of leading brands including Nest, ecobee, Honeywell, and more. A smart thermostat doesn’t just look sleek on your wall—it learns your schedule, adjusts temperatures automatically, and provides detailed energy usage reports to help you save up to 20% on heating and cooling costs. At Galico, we assess your existing HVAC system, wiring, and home layout to recommend the best smart thermostat for your needs, handle all electrical and compatibility checks, and walk you through setup and features so you get the most out of your investment. As trusted HVAC professionals across West-Ottawa, we prioritize safety, precision, and customer education—ensuring your smart thermostat works flawlessly from day one. Ready to modernize your home’s climate control? Contact Galico today for fast, reliable smart thermostat installation and start enjoying smarter comfort year-round.
