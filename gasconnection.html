Our Services

Gas Appliance Connection

At Galico, we provide safe, reliable, and code-compliant gas appliance installation and conversion services throughout Ottawa, Carleton Place, Arnprior, Perth, and Smiths Falls. Whether you’re installing a new gas cooktop or range, hooking up a gas dryer, or need a natural gas to liquid propane conversion—or vice versa—our certified gas technicians handle every job with precision, care, and full compliance with local and national safety codes. Proper gas line installation isn’t just about performance—it’s essential for your home safety and peace of mind. That’s why we perform thorough leak tests, install certified gas tags, and ensure your system runs efficiently and safely from day one. Our gas conversion services are ideal for homeowners who’ve purchased a natural gas appliance but use propane — we adjust regulators, orifices, and burners to match your fuel type perfectly. With years of experience, up-to-date certifications, and a commitment to excellent customer service, Galico makes gas appliance connections seamless, stress-free, and secure. Contact us today to schedule your service and trust your home to Ottawa-region’s dependable gas experts.
